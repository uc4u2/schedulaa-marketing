problemBlock:
  titleOne: 'The problem'
  titleTwo: 'Data scattered across multiple systems.'
  description: >
    Analytics teams needed to reconcile data from Mixpanel, internal logs, billing systems, and error trackers.
    Manual pipelines slowed down reporting and created inconsistent insights across product teams. Each tool required

    unified view meant that different teams often arrived at conflicting conclusions about user behavior, making it
    difficult to make confident product decisions based on data.
