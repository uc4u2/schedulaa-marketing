Our **AI-powered high-resolution upscaling engine** enhances image sharpness, improves textures, and restores fine details — all while preserving natural aesthetics. ## What’s included

With advanced reconstruction models and noise-reduction algorithms, you get studio-quality upscaling without manual editing or loss of fidelity.

- **4× to 8× upscaling** – Increase resolution while maintaining realism.
- **Detail reconstruction** – Restores edges, patterns, and fine textures.
- **Noise and artifact removal** – Cleans up compression damage and grain.
- **Face enhancement mode** – Improves facial features with natural precision.
- **Texture preservation** – Retains the original look without over-sharpening.
- **Multiple export formats** – PNG, JPG, TIFF, and high-resolution outputs.
