# From onboarding to advocacy

The journey from first-time user to product advocate is central to SaaS success.  
Companies that prioritize ongoing engagement and reinforcement of value see higher adoption, stronger retention, and more organic referrals.

By combining automated onboarding flows, behavioral insights, and community-driven incentives, SaaS teams can build predictable paths that transform users into long-term champions.
