# AI in product onboarding: What's Next?

AI is reshaping the onboarding experience for SaaS platforms.  
Instead of static checklists, modern onboarding systems deliver personalized paths that adjust to user behavior and intent. Recommendations surface at critical moments, helping users reach value faster.

By integrating automation, predictive insights, and real-time behavioral analysis, SaaS teams can reduce friction, increase activation rates, and create onboarding journeys that continuously learn and improve over time.
